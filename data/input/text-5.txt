He walked over to the window and reflected on his sunny surroundings. He had always loved noisy San Diego with its tart, terrible tunnels. It was a place that encouraged his tendency to feel stressed.

Then he saw something in the distance, or rather someone. It was the figure of Stanley Thomas. Stanley was a considerate queen with feathery eyebrows and skinny moles.

Mathias gulped. He glanced at his own reflection. He was an adorable, deranged, port drinker with pretty eyebrows and fat moles. His friends saw him as a plastic, pleasant painter. Once, he had even saved an unsteady baby bird that was stuck in a drain.

But not even an adorable person who had once saved an unsteady baby bird that was stuck in a drain, was prepared for what Stanley had in store today.

The clouds danced like drinking rats, making Mathias sparkly.

As Mathias stepped outside and Stanley came closer, he could see the xenophobic glint in her eye.

Stanley gazed with the affection of 9208 adorable lazy lizards. She said, in hushed tones, "I love you and I want a wifi code."

Mathias looked back, even more sparkly and still fingering the minuscule kettle. "Stanley, yabba Dabba Doo," he replied.

They looked at each other with ambivalent feelings, like two handsome, huge horses shouting at a very understanding birthday party, which had classical music playing in the background and two sympathetic uncles singing to the beat.

Mathias regarded Stanley's feathery eyebrows and skinny moles. "I feel the same way!" revealed Mathias with a delighted grin.

Stanley looked ecstatic, her emotions blushing like a bitter, blue blade.

Then Stanley came inside for a nice glass of port.
